About me

Engineer first. Leader by design.

I started writing code in 2009 and haven't stopped thinking like an engineer since. Over 14 years I've built trading platforms, sports betting systems, B2B SaaS products, and high-traffic marketing websites — across iGaming, fintech, and product companies in Malta and remotely across Europe.

Leadership found me in 2020, and I took to it deliberately. I've led squads of 7–8 senior engineers at Hotjar and Airalo, navigating a 3-way company merger, shipping at Elite DORA levels, and most importantly — fixing broken team dynamics. That's the work I find most meaningful, and where I can deliver the most value as a consultant.

I'm Malta-based, remote-native (5+ years), and fluent in the rhythms of async, distributed teams. I took a deliberate career break in 2017–18 to volunteer in third-world countries — that experience shapes how I lead: with empathy, curiosity, and a deep belief that people do their best work when they feel safe.
